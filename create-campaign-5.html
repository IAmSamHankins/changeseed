Campaign Objective

Provide school supplies (notebooks, pencils, erasers, and crayons) and backpacks for nearly 600 Honduran students in Lepaera, Honduras.

About Lepaera, Honduras

Lapaera is a beautiful town located at the foot of the Puca Mountains. The people of the town are known for being warm and friendly, and their main form of agriculture is coffee production.

The Problem

Despite the fact that Honduras can boast a 97% primary school enrollment rate, less than 40% complete their primary school education. This number is much worse for those children who are also forced to work to help support their families. While education is free for children other costs such as school supplies, backpacks, uniforms, transportation, and enrollment fees are much to expensive for many families.

The Solution

We want to do what we can to help reduce the barriers for students to getting the education they need. We hope to raise funds to provide school supplies, backpacks, and uniforms for nearly 300 school children. We have found cost effective vendors that will let us do this for approximately $9 per student. We hope you can help out.

Mission Statement

Mission Possible is a student-run organization that focuses on helping those who are less fortunate, abroad and at home.

Mission Possible leads a trip every Spring Break to complete service projects in poverty-stricken communities. Over the past three years, Mission Possible has completed community service in Honduras and offered hurricane relief assistance in New Orleans. We believe in promoting humanistic and diversity values through action and examples.
